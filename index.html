Why YouPause?

YouTube is great for learning — but it's also a trap. YouPause removes the visual triggers that keep you stuck in endless loops. With YouPause, you get what you came for — and leave.

  • Remove home page distractions
  • Hide recommended videos and Shorts
  • Disable comment sections
  • Remove trending/navigation sidebar
  • Optionally add a mindful pause
  • Whitelist genuinely useful channels

FAQ

Does YouPause block all of YouTube?

No. YouPause only hides distracting elements like recommendations, Shorts, comments, and sidebars. You can still search and watch videos as usual.

Can I whitelist certain channels?

Yes, you can maintain a list of useful channels where distractions are allowed.

What is "Mindful Pause"?

It's an optional feature that adds a small delay before playing a video to help you confirm your intent and avoid mindless clicking.

Is it free?

Yes, YouPause is completely free to use.
